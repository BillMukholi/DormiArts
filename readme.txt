Dormi Arts WordPress Theme

Dormi Arts is a custom WordPress theme specifically designed and developed for the client Dormi Arts. It is a visually stunning and feature-rich theme that caters to the needs of artists, art galleries, photographers, and creative professionals who want to showcase their work in an elegant and captivating manner.

Features
Responsive Design: The theme is fully responsive, ensuring that your website looks great on all devices and screen sizes.
Customizable Homepage: You can easily customize the theme's homepage using WordPress customizer. Add your own logo, tagline, featured content, and more.
Portfolio Showcase: Display your artwork, photography, or creative projects in a visually appealing portfolio showcase. You can organize and categorize your work using custom post types and taxonomies.
Gallery Support: The theme includes a built-in gallery feature that allows you to create stunning image galleries to showcase your work.
Blog Integration: Share your thoughts, news, and updates with your audience through the integrated blog section.
Social Media Integration: Connect with your audience by adding social media links to your website. The theme supports popular social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and more.
Contact Form: The theme includes a contact form template, making it easy for visitors to get in touch with you.
Installation
Follow the steps below to install the Dormi Arts WordPress theme:

Download the theme ZIP file from the theme repository.
Log in to your WordPress admin panel.
Navigate to "Appearance" > "Themes."
Click on the "Add New" button.
Click on the "Upload Theme" button.
Choose the ZIP file you downloaded in step 1.
Click on the "Install Now" button.
After the theme is installed, click on the "Activate" button to make it the active theme.
Once activated, you can configure the theme settings by going to "Appearance" > "Customize".
